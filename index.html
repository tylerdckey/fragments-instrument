Seasonal read. Abductive lenses, not a procedure. Write in prose; add a new dated read each season.

The seven actions (six aren't the change)

Compass Rose — shift the cost so change becomes likelier later (the default).
Monitor — just watch. Conditional — something else first. Request — needs others (liminal).
Research — needs looking into. Stabilise — it's good, hold it. Trigger — actually make the change (the only one that is).

Constraints (not Theory of Constraints)

governing / enabling · robust: rigid, elastic, tether · resilient: permeable, phase-shift, dark (you see the effect, not the cause) · connecting / containing (rewire connections first; formalise into containers only once proven).

Constructors (provisional)

designed / emergent · ritual, habit, custom & practice, role, process, catalyst · collage (parts already mean something → more) vs mosaic (meaningless parts → meaning via skill).

The metaphors (for the seasonal read)

estuary the productive mixing zone · fjordic structural and prior — shape set before the season, from above the zone; effort inside won't shift it, only change the boundary or accept the geometry (stable) · deltic dynamic and accumulative — silt from your own past commitments; don't dig it out, find and protect the live channels (progressive, shrinks if left) · compound fjordic above + deltic below = airless middle; head for the margins · salt marsh illegible boundary roles (protect, don't resolve) · flood plain overflow that enriched (don't concrete over). Fjordic and deltic need opposite moves — name which before deciding what to do. Perdurability: stay generatively engaged through disturbance rather than withdrawing into safety; robust survives, resilient returns, anti-fragile gains. Under all: what's preventing movement, and what substrate would let serendipity back in?

Zones & effect

counterfactual won't move, map around · liminal needs others (Request/Monitor) · workable yours to move.
Effect (Sanford): value-adding regenerates · sustaining holds steady · extracting depletes.

How this fits
